Privacy Policy

Cubie is built to respect your privacy. This is a plain-language summary of exactly what we store, why we store it, and the control you have over it. We keep it short because Cubie genuinely collects very little.

What we store

Two things: your account (created through Firebase Authentication — with your email and a password, with Google sign-in, or as an anonymous guest) and the content you create in the app — your habits, daily check-ins, progress, notes, and the settings you choose. That is all. Cubie does not track your location, your contacts, or anything you do outside the app.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to run Cubie for you: to show your habits and streaks, calculate your stats and achievements, deliver the reminders you turn on, and sync everything across your devices when you sign in. We never sell your data, and we never share it with advertisers.

Where your data lives

Your habits and history are stored in your own private space on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) and cached on your device so the app keeps working offline. If you use Cubie as a guest, your data stays only on your device until you link a Google account to back it up.

Notifications

Reminders and nudges are generated on your device based on your own habits and the times you set. The logic that decides when to remind you runs locally — your habit content is not sent anywhere to produce a notification.

Crash and usage reporting

In published builds, anonymous crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics) and basic, non-personal usage analytics help us find and fix problems. These reports never contain the names, notes, or content of your habits.

Purchases

If you buy Cubie Pro, the payment is handled entirely by Google Play. Cubie never sees or stores your card or banking details — we only receive confirmation from Google Play that your purchase is active.

Your control over your data

You are always in charge. You can edit or delete any habit at any time — deleting a habit also removes its history. You can export a copy of all your data from Settings, and deleting your account permanently removes your data from our servers. Signing out simply keeps your data safe with your account for next time.

Children

Cubie is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Cubie handles your data, we will update this page and the "last updated" date below. Continuing to use the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.
